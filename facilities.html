Some of the symptoms of "Delta" variant of COVID-19 are:

India's top virologists have said that the Delta Plus variant carries symptoms of the Delta as well as its partner the beta variant.

  • Cough
  • Diarrhoea
  • Fever
  • Headache
  • Skin rash
  • Discolouration of fingers and toes
  • Chest pain
  • Shortness of breath


Some of the symptoms of "Delta-plus" variant of COVID-19 are:

India's top virologists have said that the Delta Plus variant carries symptoms of the Delta as well as its partner the beta variant.

  • Stomache ache
  • Nausea
  • Appetite loss
